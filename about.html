About

My name is Divya Manian. You might know me from HTML5 Please / HTML5 Boilerplate / W3Fools / Move the web forward.

I work at Adobe. My opinions never reflect those of the company I work for.

I started earning my living by writing in Embedded C++ for Motorola, which I quickly discovered to be something I loathed. I then became an in-house designer for a startup in Singapore, and moved on to being a full-time web developer in Seattle.

I then worked at Opera as a Web Opener, and now work at Adobe as a Product Manager.

I tweet. You can reach me on email hi{at}nimbu.in
